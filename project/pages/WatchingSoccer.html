How Watching Soccer Helped Me Improve on the Field

The first game I ever watched was actually by accident. It was the 2014 World Cup final and I turned it on about 60 minutes on because there was nothing else to do. Little did I know what decision would impact my life forever as from that point on I haven't stopped watching soccer. I was a casual watcher until 2017 which was when I felt like I finally had seen enough to pick a team. The team I chose was Manchester city because Kevin De Bruyne was and still is my favorite player off all time. Watching him play actually made me better at the sport also because I could see the game from a Birds Eye view allowing me to learn from a third person perspective. This past December when Messi finally lifted the FIFA World Cup it felt like a full circle moment for me since the first match i ever watched was him losing the world cup final. Seeing the greatest player of all time finally solify himself as a god above all others was really special not to mention that world cup finally was arguably the greatest game of all time. Throughout the years of watching constant soccer I have completely transformed my on field performances and continue to do so. Now that I have in depth knowledge of the sport watching it has become even better because I can look into the plays that are happening and inform other people in my family that don't know much about it.

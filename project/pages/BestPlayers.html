The Best Players in The World

These players have personally helped me become better at the sport by watching them play. Watching these players have allowed my mind to imagine plays I thought were inconceivable before seeing them be executed on the professional stage. Most importantly these players gave me the confidence to be myself on the field and become one with the game. Below is a list of the top 25 male players of all time, top 10 female players of all time, and the current top 10 players in the world. (my opinions). The current best players have both their nation and club listed while the best players of all time have only their nation listed since most of them are retired and no longer have a club.
